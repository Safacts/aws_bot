## 📚 MEMORIZATION AIDS

### Acronyms & Mnemonics

**Six Advantages of Cloud Computing (TESGIS):**

- **T**rade CapEx for OpEx
- **E**conomies of scale
- **S**top guessing capacity
- **G**o global in minutes
- **I**ncrease speed and agility
- **S**top spending on data centers

**Well-Architected Framework (OSRPC + S):**

- **O**perational Excellence
- **S**ecurity
- **R**eliability
- **P**erformance Efficiency
- **C**ost Optimization
- **S**ustainability

**Migration Strategies (6 R's):**

- **R**ehost (Lift and Shift)
- **R**eplatform (Lift, Tinker, Shift)
- **R**epurchase (Drop and Shop)
- **R**efactor (Re-architect)
- **R**etire
- **R**etain

**Trusted Advisor Categories (CPSFS):**

- **C**ost Optimization
- **P**erformance
- **S**ecurity
- **F**ault Tolerance
- **S**ervice Limits

- Max execution time: **15 minutes**

- Durability: **99.999999999% (11 9's)**

- **750 hours/month** for 12 months

- Business (Production down): **1 hour**
